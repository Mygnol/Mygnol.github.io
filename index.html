Game Programmer | Hobbyist Artist

Long was born in Klang, Malaysia. His interest in programming started as early as 2008, on an online games platform known as Roblox. Although he was too young to use the scripting tools to their fullest at the time, the passion to create remained and still remains with him.

Now finishing his education at UOW KDU Glenmarie, majoring in Game Programming, he is all too aware of how much there is yet to learn...

...And is all too willing to give it his best shot.

Most of my code can be found on Github or Gitlab, respectively.

My interest in programming started as early as 2008, on an online games platform known as Roblox. Although I was unfortunately too young to use the scripting tools to their fullest at the time, it didn't stop me from spending countless hours playing with scripts and variables I could barely understand.

Having graduated and sunk my teeth into the games industry in general, I am happy to say that that childhood desire to see my creations come to life has not yet left me, and I am all too aware of how much there is still to learn... and am eager to find out just how much of it I can master.

When not programming, I can be found playing multiplayer co-operative games with friends, flying virtual spaceships, playing cutesy indie story games or simply drawing - allowing me to do all of the former with the help of an overactive imagination and a well-worn tablet.

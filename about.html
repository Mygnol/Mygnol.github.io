Long was born in Klang, Malaysia. His interest in programming started as early as 2008, on an online games platform known as Roblox. Although he was too young to use the scripting tools to their fullest at the time, the passion to create remained and still remains with him.

Now finishing his education at UOW KDU Glenmarie, majoring in Game Programming, he is all too aware of how much there is yet to learn... and is eager to find out how much of it he can master.

When not programming, he can be found playing multiplayer co-operative games with friends, flying virtual spaceships, building bases or simply drawing - allowing him to do all of the former with the help of an overactive imagination and a well-worn tablet.

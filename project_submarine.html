A limited information submarine simulator. The player is in control of a malfunctioning submarine trapped in a hostile cave, and can only have one system active at a time. Careful planning as well as good target prediction are essential to ensure the player makes it to the end safely, as they will be travelling blind if they wish to travel at all.

Game Engine: Unity Engine

Language Used: C#

Group Size: 3 (3 Programmers)

My Role(s): Programmer/Artist
